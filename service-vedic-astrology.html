What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic Astrology is rooted in the ancient texts of the Vedas, the oldest scriptures of Hinduism. Unlike Western astrology which uses the tropical zodiac, Vedic astrology employs the sidereal zodiac, which accounts for the precession of the equinoxes and aligns with the actual constellation positions in the sky.

The foundation of Vedic astrology lies in the concept of karma - the law of cause and effect. Your birth chart, or Kundali, is a map of your karmic patterns, showing both the fruits of past actions and the potential for future growth. By understanding these patterns, you gain the power to work with cosmic energies rather than against them.

What's Included in a Vedic Astrology Consultation?

Complete Birth Chart Analysis

Your birth chart is prepared using precise astronomical calculations based on your date, time, and place of birth. I analyze all nine planets (Grahas), twelve houses (Bhavas), twelve zodiac signs (Rashis), and 27 lunar mansions (Nakshatras) to create a comprehensive picture of your life's potential.

Planetary Period Analysis (Dasha System)

The Vimshottari Dasha system is unique to Vedic astrology and reveals which planetary periods you are currently experiencing. This incredibly accurate system explains why certain themes dominate different periods of your life and helps predict future trends with remarkable precision.

Divisional Chart Analysis

Beyond the main birth chart, I examine various divisional charts (Vargas) that provide detailed insights into specific life areas such as career (D10), marriage (D9), education (D24), and wealth (D2). This multi-layered analysis offers unprecedented depth and accuracy.

Transit Analysis

Current planetary movements are analyzed to understand how they interact with your birth chart. This helps identify auspicious periods for new beginnings and challenging times that require caution and preparedness.
