Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about consultations and services

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation by calling us at 09014056713, using the contact form above, or messaging us on WhatsApp. We will schedule an appointment based on your convenience and availability.

What should I bring for the consultation?

Please bring your accurate birth details (date, time, and place of birth). For Kundali matching, both partners' birth details are required. For Vastu consultation, bring your property layout or floor plan.

How long does a consultation take?

A typical consultation lasts 45-60 minutes. Complex cases like detailed birth chart analysis or Vastu consultation may take longer. We ensure adequate time to address all your concerns.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer phone consultations for clients who cannot visit in person. The accuracy and quality of guidance remain the same whether you consult in person or over the phone.

What are your consultation fees?

Consultation fees vary based on the service required. Please call us for detailed pricing information. We ensure our services are affordable and provide value for your investment.

How accurate are the predictions?

We have a strong track record of accurate predictions, as testified by thousands of satisfied clients. However, astrology works with probabilities and cosmic energies. We provide the best possible guidance based on your chart.

Do you offer remedies for problems?

Yes, we provide comprehensive remedial solutions including gemstone recommendations, mantras, charitable activities, Vastu corrections, and spiritual practices. All remedies are simple, practical, and effective.

Is the consultation confidential?

Absolutely! We maintain complete confidentiality of all information shared during consultations. Your personal details and concerns are treated with utmost privacy and respect.
