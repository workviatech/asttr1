Discover Marriage Compatibility Through Kundali Matching

Kundali Matching, also known as Horoscope Matching or Kundli Milan, is an ancient Vedic astrology practice used to assess the compatibility between prospective marriage partners. This time-tested method analyzes the birth charts of both individuals to determine their physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual compatibility, ensuring a harmonious and prosperous married life.

Marriage is a sacred union that binds two souls for life. Through comprehensive Kundali Matching, I help families and couples make one of life's most important decisions with confidence and clarity. My detailed analysis goes beyond the traditional point system to provide deep insights into relationship dynamics and potential challenges.

What is Kundali Matching?

Kundali Matching is based on the Ashtakoot system, which analyzes eight different aspects (Kootas) of compatibility between two birth charts. Each aspect represents different dimensions of married life, from physical attraction to spiritual harmony. The system awards a maximum of 36 points (Gunas), with higher scores indicating better compatibility.

The Eight Kootas Analyzed

1. Varna (1 point) - Spiritual Compatibility

Analyzes the spiritual development and ego levels of partners. Ensures mutual respect and understanding in the relationship.

2. Vashya (2 points) - Magnetic Control & Attraction

Determines mutual attraction and influence between partners. Indicates who will have more influence in decision-making.

3. Tara (3 points) - Birth Star Compatibility

Based on the Nakshatra (lunar constellation) compatibility. Affects overall health, wellbeing, and longevity of the relationship.

4. Yoni (4 points) - Sexual & Physical Compatibility

Assesses physical and sexual compatibility between partners. Crucial for intimate harmony in marriage.

5. Graha Maitri (5 points) - Psychological Compatibility

Evaluates mental compatibility, friendship, and understanding. Determines if partners can be best friends.

6. Gana (6 points) - Temperament Matching

Analyzes behavioral patterns and temperament. Ensures compatibility in lifestyle and habits.

7. Bhakoot (7 points) - Family Welfare & Prosperity

Predicts family happiness, prosperity, and progeny. Crucial for financial and family growth.

8. Nadi (8 points) - Health & Genetic Compatibility

Most important factor assessing health compatibility and genetic compatibility of future children. Nadi dosha can cause serious health issues and must be carefully analyzed.

Understanding Doshas & Remedies

Manglik Dosha (Mangal Dosha)

Occurs when Mars is placed in 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house. Can cause delays, conflicts, or marital discord. Remedies include Mangal Shanti Puja, fasting on Tuesdays, wearing red coral gemstone, and marrying another Manglik person.

Nadi Dosha

The most serious dosha, occurring when both partners have the same Nadi (Aadi, Madhya, or Antya). Can cause health issues and progeny problems. Requires careful analysis as some exceptions cancel this dosha. Remedies include Maha Mrityunjaya Japa and specific pujas.

Bhakoot Dosha

Arises from incompatible Moon sign combinations. Affects family prosperity and happiness. Remedies include Chandra Puja and charity activities.

Gana Dosha

Temperament mismatch between Deva, Manushya, and Rakshasa Gana. Can cause personality clashes. Remedies include behavioral adjustments and understanding exercises.
